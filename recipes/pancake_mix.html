Homemade Pancake Mix

Description

Who doesn't like a fresh homemade flapjack. But in case you've forgotten how to make your mix, here's how!

Ingredients

  • 5 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups powdered milk
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt

    • Directions

    1. Combine flour, powdered milk, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl, and mix well.
    2. Prepare and store mixture in an airtight container, use within 8 months.
    3. To make 8 4-in pancakes, use 2 cups of pancake mix, along with 1 cup of water, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of oil.
